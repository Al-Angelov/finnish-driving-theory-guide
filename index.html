How to Pass the Test

To pass the Finnish B-class driver's theory test, study traffic rules, understand all road signs, and practice regularly. Focus on hazard detection, right-of-way rules, and updated signage. Use the resources below to prepare efficiently.

üéØ The Theory Test: Structure & Rules

  • 15 multiple-choice questions (3 options, 1 correct)
  • 50 "traffic situation" questions (animated scenarios)
  • 5 hazard-identification questions

Time limit: 30 minutes. Extended time (60 min) available for non-native English speakers or medical reasons.

Passing Criteria: You must pass all 3 parts in one sitting. Very few mistakes are allowed.

Retake Validity: Your theory pass is valid for 1 year.

Language: Available in English. CAP offers English subtitles and lessons.

  • Traffic rules (e.g. right of way, speed limits, overtaking)
  • Driver responsibilities & obligations
  • Vehicle operation and eco-driving
  • Traffic signs and markings
  • Hazard and risk identification

  • Request extended time or a verbal test if needed
